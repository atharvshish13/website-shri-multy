◆ About Us ◆

14+ years turning roadsides, shopfronts
and farm-shows into brand moments

Shri Multy Services is an advertising material provider based in Amravati, Maharashtra. What started as a local branding outfit has grown into a full-service partner for vehicle branding, outdoor signage, shop branding, and agri-sector events — trusted by agrochemical, seed and FMCG majors across Central India.

Led by founder Tushar Diwan, our team handles everything end-to-end: design, fabrication, installation and on-ground event execution — so clients get one point of contact for every branding need.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted, result-driven advertising partner across Central India — where every brand we touch becomes impossible to miss.

Our Mission

To deliver creative, reliable and result-oriented advertising services, combining field visibility with long-term client partnerships.
